# Stellarae  
### Advancing the Future Through Open Source Excellence

At **Stellarae**, we are committed to shaping a future where open‑source technology empowers individuals, strengthens communities, and accelerates global innovation. We believe that transparency, collaboration, and shared knowledge form the foundation of meaningful technological progress.

## Our Mission

To design, develop, and steward high‑quality open‑source software that enables creators, developers, and organizations to build with confidence. We aim to reduce barriers to technology, promote equitable access to tools and knowledge, and cultivate an environment where innovation thrives.

## Our Vision

A world where open‑source software is the backbone of technological advancement—where collaboration transcends borders, and where every developer has the opportunity to contribute, learn, and create lasting impact.

## Why Open Source?

- **Transparency** — Open code builds trust, accountability, and long‑term reliability.  
- **Collaboration** — Global contributions strengthen ideas and accelerate progress.  
- **Innovation** — Diverse perspectives drive rapid iteration and breakthrough solutions.  
- **Accessibility** — Free and open tools ensure that technology remains available to all.

## Our Commitment

- **Open Source, Always** — Every project we create or maintain remains open and accessible.  
- **Community First** — We prioritize community needs, feedback, and participation.  
- **Excellence in Quality** — Strong standards for documentation, security, and maintainability.  
- **Inclusive Culture** — Welcoming contributors of all backgrounds and experience levels.

## Contribution Policy

To maintain the integrity and security of our ecosystem, contributions to sensitive repositories require verified signatures. This includes systems with elevated privileges, infrastructure libraries, and any assets representing the Stellarae brand.  
For all other repositories, reviews remain mandatory, and signed commits are encouraged to help ensure contributor authenticity.

## Looking Ahead

Stellarae is continually evolving. Our long‑term focus includes:

- Strengthening compliance and standardization across our software  
- Improving distribution through widely used package managers  
- Providing consistent, reliable installation experiences across platforms
